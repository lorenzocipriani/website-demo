What truly defines each Latte e Biscotti collection is the exceptional quality of our fabrics, our exquisite choice of colours and, above all, our uniqueness. All of these represent what is sought by more and more mums nowadays who are looking for traditional yet innovative styles.
The Collection combines both fashion and comfort in a contemporary way.

Latte e Biscotti has been and will continue to be an exemplar of elegance and minimalism with a difference.

The company is based in Umbria (the green heart of Italy) and it is here that each design is lovingly brought to life by our unique team of “artisans” who every day pour passion & enthusiasm into creating our special garments.
