Delivery terms and conditions

You must make sure there is somebody to accept your delivery to the address provided and you must provide a day time telephone number that we can contact you on. If we are unable to deliver after two telephone calls we will notify you to arrange an alternative date for delivery. Post and Packaging are fixed costs and therefore will not be refunded. Latte e Biscotti will not be held responsible for items damaged or lost whilst being returned to us. We reserve the right not to accept an order.
